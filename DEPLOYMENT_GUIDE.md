# 🚀 WoundCare AI - Deployment Guide

## 🎯 **Multiple Deployment Options**

Your WoundCare AI application can be deployed to **any static hosting platform** since it's now a pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript application with no server-side dependencies.

## 📋 **Option 1: GitHub Pages (Recommended)**

### **Step 1: Push to GitHub**
1. Your code is already on GitHub at `nadia-jelani/wound`
2. Make sure you're on the `clean-branch`

### **Step 2: Enable GitHub Pages**
1. Go to your repository: `https://github.com/nadia-jelani/wound`
2. Click **"Settings"** tab
3. Scroll down to **"Pages"** section
4. Under **"Source"**, select **"Deploy from a branch"**
5. Choose **"clean-branch"** and **"/ (root)"**
6. Click **"Save"**

### **Step 3: Get Your URL**
- Your app will be available at: `https://nadia-jelani.github.io/wound/`
- Takes 2-3 minutes to deploy

## 📋 **Option 2: Netlify (Free & Fast)**

### **Step 1: Go to Netlify**
1. Visit **https://netlify.com**
2. Sign up with your **GitHub account**
3. Click **"New site from Git"**

### **Step 2: Connect Repository**
1. Choose **GitHub**
2. Select repository: **`nadia-jelani/wound`**
3. Branch: **`clean-branch`**
4. Build command: **Leave empty** (no build needed)
5. Publish directory: **Leave empty** (root directory)

### **Step 3: Deploy**
1. Click **"Deploy site"**
2. Your app will be live in 30 seconds!
3. URL will be: `https://random-name.netlify.app`

## 📋 **Option 3: Vercel (Free & Fast)**

### **Step 1: Go to Vercel**
1. Visit **https://vercel.com**
2. Sign up with your **GitHub account**
3. Click **"New Project"**

### **Step 2: Import Repository**
1. Select repository: **`nadia-jelani/wound`**
2. Framework Preset: **"Other"**
3. Root Directory: **`./`**
4. Build Command: **Leave empty**
5. Output Directory: **Leave empty**

### **Step 3: Deploy**
1. Click **"Deploy"**
2. Your app will be live in 30 seconds!
3. URL will be: `https://wound-nadia-jelani.vercel.app`

## 📋 **Option 4: Render Static Site**

### **Step 1: Go to Render**
1. Visit **https://render.com**
2. Sign up with your **GitHub account**
3. Click **"New +"** → **"Static Site"**

### **Step 2: Configure**
1. Connect repository: **`nadia-jelani/wound`**
2. Branch: **`clean-branch`**
3. Build Command: **Leave empty**
4. Publish Directory: **Leave empty**

### **Step 3: Deploy**
1. Click **"Create Static Site"**
2. Your app will be live in 1 minute!
3. URL will be: `https://woundcare-ai.onrender.com`
